About SZABIST MART

Welcome to SZABIST MART, your trusted online grocery store dedicated to bringing fresh, quality products directly to your doorstep. Founded by Ayush and Keshav, we believe in providing an excellent shopping experience with the best prices and exceptional customer service.

Our mission is simple: to make grocery shopping convenient, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone. We carefully select every product to ensure quality and freshness, partnering with trusted suppliers and farmers.

Whether you're looking for fresh vegetables, premium dairy, exotic spices, or pantry essentials, SZABIST MART has everything you need. With 24/7 customer support and fast delivery, we're committed to making your shopping experience seamless.

Our Core Values:

  • ✓ Quality First: We never compromise on product quality
  • ✓ Customer Focused: Your satisfaction is our priority
  • ✓ Fair Prices: Affordable without compromising quality
  • ✓ Sustainability: Environmentally conscious practices

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver outside Karachi?

Currently, we deliver within Karachi. We're expanding to other cities soon. Stay tuned!

What is your return policy?

We offer easy returns within 7 days of purchase. If any product is defective or not as described, we'll replace it for free.

Do you offer bulk orders?

Yes! We offer special discounts for bulk orders. Please contact our support team for more details.

How do I track my order?

You'll receive a tracking link via email once your order is dispatched. You can track your delivery in real-time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, bank transfers, JazzCash, and cash on delivery. All payments are secure and encrypted.
